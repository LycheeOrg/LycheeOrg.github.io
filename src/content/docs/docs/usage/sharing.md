Lychee provides flexible sharing options to control who can see and interact with your photos.

## Public Sharing

Make albums publicly accessible so anyone with the link can view them. Public albums can optionally:

- Require a password for access
- Allow or restrict downloads
- Be listed on the public gallery page

## User-Based Sharing

Share albums with specific registered users on your Lychee instance:

- Grant read-only or full access
- Cross-user sharing allows collaboration between users
- Per-album access control ensures fine-grained permissions

## Embedding

Albums can be embedded in external websites, allowing you to showcase your photos on blogs, portfolios, or other sites.

## Sensitive Albums

Mark albums as sensitive to hide them behind an additional click, useful for content that should not appear in public feeds or previews.

## Link Sharing

Generate shareable links for albums. With the [Supporter Edition](/docs/se/overview/), you can also use:

- **AES-secured links** — encrypted links that prevent URL tampering. See [AES-Secured Links](/docs/se/aes-links/).
