Protect your photos by applying custom watermarks with the Supporter Edition.

## Features

- **Custom image watermark** — use your own logo or image as a watermark
- **Customizable positioning** — place the watermark anywhere on the photo
- **Adjustable scaling** — control the watermark size relative to the photo
- **Opacity control** — set the watermark transparency level

## Configuration

Upload your watermark image and configure its placement in the Lychee settings panel. Watermarks are applied to generated photo variants while preserving the original file.
