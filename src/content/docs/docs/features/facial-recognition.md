:::note
Facial recognition is planned for Lychee v8 and is currently in development.
:::

Lychee will include facial recognition capabilities to automatically detect and group photos by the people in them.

## Planned Features

- Automatic face detection in uploaded photos
- Face grouping and person identification
- Browse photos by person
- Manual face tagging and corrections
